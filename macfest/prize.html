Prize package!

This year MacFEST has added a new dimension to the festival: a band contest! Run in co-ordination with the Bear 106.9 and Distortion Studios, bands will submit their press kits and demos for consideration as performers at MacFEST.

The top three bands will perform at MacFEST, and the top band will win a recording package from Distortion Studios!

Distortion Studio Inc. will donate 6 hours (with engineer) in Distortion Studio 1 (a value of $450.00). Keep a listen to the BEAR (106.9FM) to find out how to win!
